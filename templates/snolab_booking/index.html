SNOLAB Visit and apartment/room booking system

Generally, apartment rooms are prioritised for their respective national experiment collaboration members. If there is availability, rooms will be offered to others at a rate of 25 $CAN per night (this rate also applies to non-department funded UK students). Further surcharges may apply if priority visitors on short-notice trips are displaced. Addition restrictions may also apply depending on the specific choice of apartment. Invoicing for room charges will be organised by the appropriate UK, US or Canadian managing institution.
